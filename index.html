Our Approach

At Kriya, we bring together diverse stakeholders to address pressing challenges faced by communities and the environment. Our approach is built on collaboration and innovation, involving:

  • Government Officials: Identify and submit real-world problems that need innovative solutions. Their insights and expertise are crucial in framing the challenges that our society faces today.
  • Solution Providers: Comprising startups, corporates, students, universities, and researchers, this group brings forward cutting-edge solutions and technologies to tackle the problems identified by government officials. They are the backbone of our innovative efforts, turning ideas into actionable solutions.
  • Funders: Financial institutions and philanthropic organizations provide the necessary funding to support the implementation of these solutions. Their role is instrumental in turning viable solutions into reality, ensuring that the projects have the resources needed for success.
  • Kriya Team: Our team plays a pivotal role in coordinating between government officials, solution providers, and funders. We ensure that the problems are accurately matched with the best solutions and that the projects receive the funding they require. We also facilitate communication and collaboration among all parties to ensure the seamless execution of projects.

Together, we strive to create a sustainable impact, leveraging technology and innovation to solve complex challenges and improve lives.
